Formlabs

Formlabs is a Somerville, Massachusetts-based company that was founded in September 2011. Formlabs designs and manufactures desktop 3D printers, and is well known for raising nearly $3 million in a Kickstarter campaign and creating the Form 1 and Form 2 3D printers.
Wikipedia

Form 2

The most advanced desktop 3D printer ever created. Industrial quality 3D printing made accessible, the Form 2 delivers high-resolution prints at a fraction of the cost. Discover what you can create with desktop stereolithography (SLA) by requesting a sample part. Go!

Fuse 1

On 5 June 2017, Formlabs announced the Fuse 1, a selective laser sintering 3D printer. It has a much larger build volume, a removable build chamber, and uses nylon powder. Go!

Form Cell

On 5 June 2017, Formlabs announced the Form Cell, a cell of Form 2 3D printers, as well as a Form Wash and a robotic gantry system. It is completely automated and can be used as a 24 hour digital “factory”. Go!
